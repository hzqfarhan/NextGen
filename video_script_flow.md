# NextGen: Video Flow and Script

This document provides a structured video flow and script designed for a 3 to 5-minute video presentation, adhering to the AIoTIE 2026 competition guidelines and strictly utilizing features documented in the `extendedabstract.md`.

## ⏱️ Video Structure Overview
- **0:00 - 0:45**: Introduction & Problem Statement
- **0:45 - 1:15**: Proposed Solution
- **1:15 - 2:45**: Demonstration of Functionality (Key Features)
- **2:45 - 3:30**: Explanation of Innovative Product (AI Architecture)
- **3:30 - 4:00**: Cost Effectiveness & Benefit to Mankind
- **4:00 - 4:30**: Commercialization Potential & Conclusion
